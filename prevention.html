How to prevent scamming

1. Verify Communications

Always verify the authenticity of any communication claiming to be from your bank. Contact your bank directly using official contact details.

2. Be Cautious with Personal Information

Never share your bank details, passwords, or PINs with anyone.

3. Use Security Features

Enable two-factor authentication and other security features offered by your bank.

4. Stay Informed

Keep up-to-date with common scams and how to recognize them.

5. Report Suspicious Activity

If you suspect you've been targeted by a scam, report it to your bank immediately.
