TYPES OF SCAMMING

Clients are tricked into authorizing a payment to a scammer's account, believing they are paying a legitimate entity.

This includes unauthorized transactions made using stolen card details, either online or in physical stores.

Scammers gain access to a client's bank account through stolen credentials and make unauthorized transactions.

Clients are lured into fake investment opportunities, often involving large sums of money, which are then stolen by the scammers.

Fraudsters offer fake loans requiring upfront fees, which are never refunded once the loan is supposedly approved.
